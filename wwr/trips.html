Who Are We?

We are Gravity Adventures, a once-in-a-lifetime athletic service that offers white water rafting. Our goal is to provide our rafters a memorable and safe experience. Our business was founded in the 1980s, just following the Olympics. Since then, we have been offering thrills and family enjoyment to anybody brave enough to cross the Ocoee River.

Where Are We Located?

Our home is located near the Ocoee River in mid-state Tennessee. Are you curious about how to get there? Check out our Contact Us page.

What Should I Bring?

Given that you will be getting wet and spending many hours in the sun, you might wish to wear a swimsuit, water shoes, and sunscreen. Lunch will be given if you are participating for the entire day. When you're out on the river, please don't bring devices since you could tip and lose them.
